OUR MISSION STATEMENT

We aim to create a tool students can use throughout their studies as a source of motivation that will increase their productivity. No doubt, school is stressful, and it can lead to more distress than eustress. To counter this problem, our website will provide resources for students to avert or overcome burnouts and slumps to maintain their mental health.

ABOUT OUR NAME

We named our website r.ripple because it is visible through students that the American education system is causing a ripple effect in them, one that is negative and not positive. We want to create a reverse ripple effect and lend support to those under stress in school. Moreover, r.ripple is a play on words as its pronunciation sounds like "our ripple," which symbolizes our hope that our website can be of use to students in middle school and beyond. It represents our impact, too.
